Course Information

Detailed information about the course, textbook, schedule is provided in the syllabus here.

Course development history: This course was developed by Dr. Ian Muehenlenhaus in 2014 when he was a Geography professor here. The course have been adapted over the years to teach most current mapping technologies in the industry. Reading materials include Dr. Muehenlenhaus's text book "Webmapping" and the web book "Introduction to Webmapping" by Michael Dorman. The labs and exercises were developed, revised, adapted by the current instructor. Majority of the exercises are adapted or used as is from Dorman's web book. Labs 1, and 2 are adapted froms original labs developed by Dr. Muehenlenhaus. Labs 6 is adapted from Dr. Ting Liu at Northeastern Illinois University.
